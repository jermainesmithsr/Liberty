Liberty Community Empowerment

In any community-centered economic ecosystem, a local source of financing and capital is vital to ensure that responsible community development is prioritized. Liberty Community Empowerment identifies financing and structures to acquire and build affordable housing; it also creates and delivers loan products for homeowners and entrepreneurs, and develops community wealth building opportunities in a non-extractive, responsible manner. Eventually, Liberty Community Empowerment plans to evolve into the Liberty Impact Fund, providing the above services, as well as Certified Community Development Entity, CDFI certification (lending), EB-5 Regional Center certification and Liberty Opportunity Fund (opportunity zone fund).
