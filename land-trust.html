Liberty Community Land Trust

Liberty Community Land Trust (LCLT) is the only Black-led, Black focused community land trust operating in California. While partner organization Downtown Crenshaw Rising works to acquire and redevelop the Baldwin Hills Crenshaw Plaza, LCLT is focused on acquiring housing in the areas surrounding the mall to preserve affordable housing. In the long term, LCLT plans to transition tenants into owners of housing cooperatives while continuing to acquire and convert market rate spaces into affordable housing. Liberty Community Land Trust has received funding from the County of LA Pilot Program, Liberty Hill Foundation, Weingart Foundation, James Irvine Foundation and Chan Zuckerberg Initiative.
